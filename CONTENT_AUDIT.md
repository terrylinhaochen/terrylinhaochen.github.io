## Placeholder Mapping

Use these briefs as the backlog for new content. The goal is not to polish filler; it is to replace each placeholder with an actual claim.

- `content/posts/agent_economy.md` -> `The Agentic Economy`: what changes when agents become persistent workers rather than tools. Needs examples of org structure, specs, ownership, and supervision.
- `content/posts/google_notebooklm.md` -> `NotebookLM and grounded knowledge work`: why source-grounded AI feels different from chat, with a concrete workflow.
- `content/posts/search_topic.md` -> `Search becomes topic infrastructure`: how search changes when agents, not humans, are the primary consumers.
- `content/posts/xiaomi-primer.md` -> `Xiaomi as ecosystem execution`: not a company profile; a note on hardware, distribution, and software bundling.
- `content/posts/google_primer.md` -> `Google as the AI distribution layer`: what people understate about Google's default surfaces, and where that advantage may fail.
- `content/posts/security.md` -> `Security data pipelines and agentic SOC`: explain the shift from SIEM cost pain to data routing, normalization, and agent-assisted investigation.
- `content/posts/sunsetting_projects.md` -> `When to stop building`: first-person post using Cogno, Marrrket, A2A Catalog, and CrowdListen to separate product failure from distribution failure.
- `content/posts/interesting_reads.md` -> `What Manus taught me about agent products`: convert the transcript into an argument about wrappers, UX, evals, and long-horizon execution.
- `content/posts/monetizing_ai.md` -> `AI monetization is willingness to pay, not capability`: use TikTok Symphony/CrowdListen examples to show why model magic does not automatically create budget.
- `content/posts/crowdlisten_archive_v2025_09_01.md` -> no rewrite needed; keep as source history only because `crowdlisten_archive_v2025_11_09.md` is the stronger archived snapshot.
- `content/posts/crypto_valuation.md` -> `Why crypto valuation still feels unfalsifiable`: expand the honest starting point into a stronger note about what valuation methods would need to prove.
