## Developing capable multi-agent systems for complex reasoning and human-AI collaboration

Single-agent AI systems have limitations in handling complex tasks that require diverse perspectives and specialized knowledge. My research focuses on multi-agent architectures that enable more sophisticated reasoning, problem-solving, and collaboration capabilities.

### Key Research Areas:

1. **Agent specialization and coordination**
   - Designing agents with complementary capabilities
   - Creating efficient communication protocols between agents

2. **Complex reasoning in collaborative environments**
   - Breaking down problems into sub-tasks for specialized agents
   - Synthesizing diverse agent outputs into coherent solutions

3. **Human-AI team dynamics**
   - Optimizing the division of labor between humans and AI agents
   - Developing intuitive interfaces for multi-agent collaboration

This work aims to expand the capabilities of AI systems by mimicking the collaborative intelligence seen in human teams, enabling more sophisticated problem-solving and decision-making processes.
