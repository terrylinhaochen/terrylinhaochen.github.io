## Competitor keyword gap (Lenny ecosystem framing)

### What competitors are strong on

1. **Template-heavy PM intent** (e.g., PRD templates, one-pagers, PM docs) is already captured by Lenny ecosystem content and large PM education domains.
2. **General “AI for PM” listicles** are crowded (Product School, tool blogs, consulting content).
3. **Broad roadmap/prioritization terms** are dominated by product platform players (e.g., Productboard, Aha!, Atlassian-adjacent ecosystems).

### Where you can win (gap)

1. **“Signal → Spec” workflow terms** are underserved in one coherent narrative:  
   - `user feedback synthesis ai`  
   - `voice of customer to prd`  
   - `agent-ready spec`
2. **Practical anti-fluff positioning** for PMs burned by generic AI advice:  
   - `how to write prd with ai` with explicit failure modes + quality checks.
3. **Agentic PM execution layer** (not just ideation):  
   - `spec writing ai` + `roadmap automation` + `ai product manager workflow` tied together as one operating system.

### Positioning angle to differentiate from Lenny-adjacent incumbents

- Many incumbents teach **what good PM looks like**.  
- Your edge can be showing **how PMs operationalize AI end-to-end on real inputs** (social signal, interviews, support tickets) and produce **build-ready outputs**.
