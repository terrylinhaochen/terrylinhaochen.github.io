2024 felt to have passed by very quickly, in part because I had interesting work to occupy my time. Early in the year, after expanding Cogno and gaining some traction, work slowly stalled: though we did code and talk to customers, the cycles were far and wide in between. The focus on multi-agent systems was (as of writing this) in the right direction, yet we did not find the niche to tackle sales conversion improvement. Dropping out is not what I want at this stage of life - I still wanted the complete college experience. 

In March, after a year of working on Cogno, I decided to go to TikTok to build agent systems for Creative Copilots and Insights there. I flew from San Juan to San Jose, cutting short my long anticipated vacation. This turned out to have been worth it, allowing me to take a first row seat in observing how LLMs and Agents play out. The work on insight extraction from multimodal data was interesting, yet I'm still trying to wrap my mind around what kind of product would most directly benefit from this. 

Later in the year, going to Beijing, and heading back to school felt like a blur. But I turned out to enjoy school a lot more this quarter, in part due to pretty amazing teams and project (LLM focused) based classes. There seemed to be an element of focus less commonly seen in industry. I continued working on content understanding through Crowdlistening (Work in Progress, still displayed as in stealth on Linkedin) and started working on a new project (Still in Stealth). I look forward to seeing how these play out.
