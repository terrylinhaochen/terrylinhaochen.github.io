"Inspiration unlocks the future, technology will catch up." This is a phrase by one of my favorite directors Hayao Miyazaki in the animation "The Wind Rises" (Though my favorite movie is Princess Mononoke). While it's easy to get caught up in the innovation (and the fluff of hype popular news outlets creates along side it), I still view technology as a tool to be used in solving human problems. This may sound contradictory, yet most of us are still pretty bad at identifying our own needs and pains, therefore the engineering feats with non-existent PMF prospects. Going back to the question of why I want to start writing again: I like the language medium, it forces you to express your ideas clearly. I anticipate mainly writing about products and the strange quirks that make us human, but maybe at times also add in a few other things that interest me. I'll try to keep these posts relatively short, both to make it manageable for myself, and to keep it interesting for you, the readers. Happy reading.
